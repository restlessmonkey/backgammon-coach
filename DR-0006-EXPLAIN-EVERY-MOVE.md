# DR-0006 — Explain Every Move

## Status
Implemented in v1.9.

## Decision
Each manually selected human move is compared against the locally ranked legal choices available immediately before the move.

## User-facing output
- Rating from Perfect through Blunder.
- Plain-English strategic explanation.
- Safety, Attack, Flexibility, and Race meters.
- Comparison with the coach's highest-ranked legal sequence.
- Optional saved Favorite Lesson.
- End-of-game aggregate Coach Report.

## Safety boundary
The rankings are generated by the app's transparent teaching heuristic. They are not represented as neural-network or professional backgammon-engine equity calculations.
