Backgammon basics

You move your checkers in opposite directions around the board. Use both dice when legally possible. A single opposing checker is a blot and may be hit. Two or more opposing checkers make a closed point.

Checkers on the bar must re-enter before any other checker can move. Once all 15 checkers are in your home board, you may bear them off.
