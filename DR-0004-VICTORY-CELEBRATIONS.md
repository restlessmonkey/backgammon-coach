# DR-0004 — Victory Celebrations

## Decision
A human win triggers fireworks followed by one randomly selected checker animation. The five finales are Rocket Checkers, Checker Hill, Classic Bounce, Victory Vortex, and Checker Fountain.

## Safety and control
- A visible Stop celebration button is always available.
- Escape also stops the animation.
- Starting a new game stops any active animation.
- Reduced-motion preferences receive a short, low-motion celebration.
- The effect is decorative and does not modify game state.
